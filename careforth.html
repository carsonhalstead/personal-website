Careforth

About

Roughly one in four American adults, an estimated 63 million, are currently caregivers. These are the stewards of wellbeing in children or adults who are sick, elderly, or living with disabilities.

I’m on a team of two Digital Product Designers at Careforth. For the past four years, I’ve been responsible for the experience of a suite of digital platforms. I partner with leadership to build and maintain tools that connect caregivers with support, education, and resources.

To learn more about selected projects at Careforth, contact me.
